Staff

Dr. Brigham Stoker, DDS

Salt Lake City dentist, Dr. Brigham Stoker, truly enjoys his work. Striving to provide the best dental experience possible, Dr. Stoker is committed to treating each patient who visits our dental practice with the highest standard of care. You will be impressed with his ability to make you feel comfortable and at ease as he helps you make important decisions about your oral health. He feels fortunate to have graduated from the University of North Carolina School of Dentistry, where he received an outstanding dental education from some of the best mentors in the country. Prior to dental school he attended the University of Utah, where he earned a B.S. in Physics and Mathematics Education, and a M.S. in Physics Instrumentation. During that time he owned and operated Think! Tutoring, Inc., helping secondary students through their own education experience. Dr. Stoker currently resides in Bountiful, Utah with his wife and 4 children.
